Generate CA Private Key:
`openssl genrsa -out ca.key.pem 2048`

Generate CA Certificate:
`openssl req -new -x509 -key ca.key.pem -out ca.cert.pem`
Enter "." for all fields except for Common Name, which should be "g4_secure_drop"

Look at CA Certificate:
`openssl x509 -noout -text -in ca.cert.pem`

Generate client Private Key:
`openssl genrsa -out client1.key.pem 2048`

Generate client CSR (Request):
`openssl req -key client1.key.pem -new -sha256 -out client1.csr.pem`
Enter "." for all fields except for Common Name and Email address, which should both be "client1@foo.com"

Generate client Certificate using CA:
`openssl x509 -req -in client1.csr.pem -CA ca.cert.pem -CAkey ca.key.pem -CAcreateserial -out client1.cert.pem`

Look at client Certificate:
`openssl x509 -noout -text -in client1.cert.pem`

Verify that client Certificate was signed by CA:
`openssl verify -CAfile ca.cert.pem client1.cert.pem`
